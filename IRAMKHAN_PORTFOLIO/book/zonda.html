Pagani Zonda F

The Pagani Zonda F was introduced in 2005 at the Geneva Motor Show. It is an evolution of the Zonda C12, featuring a 7.3L Mercedes-Benz AMG V12 engine. With a lightweight carbon fiber body, it delivers extreme performance and precision handling.

Pagani Zonda Cinque

The Zonda Cinque is a limited edition supercar, with only five units ever produced. It features a 678 hp AMG V12 engine, carbon-titanium chassis, and unmatched exclusivity.

Pagani Zonda R

Built purely for the track, the Zonda R has a 6.0L V12 producing 750 hp. It is a masterpiece of aerodynamics, lightweight design, and raw power — a true track weapon not intended for road use.
