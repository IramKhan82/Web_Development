Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence by computer systems, enabling machines to learn from data, reason, solve problems, and perform tasks like language understanding, decision-making, and pattern recognition. It focuses on building systems that can act, learn, and adapt independently, commonly categorized into Narrow (ANI), General (AGI), and Super Intelligence (ASI)
